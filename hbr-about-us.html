Our Story


American Frontier Riders is your official outfitter for an unparalleled Glacier National Park ride on horseback.

Our horseback riding experiences take place on the scenic american frontier, where our horse-powered transportation offers the riders a chance to witness the untamed Montana scenery from a whole new perspective, as we border the legendary mountain-sides of Glacier National Park from a new angle.

From first timers to riding pros, we have an amercian fronetier rider experience suited for just about anyone on the trials. Follow the lead of Glacier’s best guides as you ride through forests, rivers, mountains, etc. as you take in the spectacular views and discover the wonders of the trials on the adventure that’s right for you.

Discover more about our collection of possibilities to adventure with us today, so you can witness first hand the frontier fun with Glacier’s most well-known horseback riding company.

Mission Statement

When was the last time that you explored the American Frontier on the saddle of a majestic horse? Did you feel that satisfaction that comes from being an American? Here at American Frontier Riders, we provide you with an experience of a lifetime by creating a true bond between horse and man as you both cross the American Frontier through our various trails. Because “The history of mankind is carried on the back of a horse.”
