The Best Horseback Riding In The Frontier

Rafting Rodeo is your official outfitter for an unparalleled Glacier National Park amphibious ride.

Our rafting rodeo experiences takes place on the scenic frontier river-tops of the North Fork, Middle Fork and South Fork of the Flathead River. Our paddle-powered adventures offer the riders a chance to witness the untamed Montana scenery from a whole new perspective, as we border the legendary frontier of Glacier National Park from a new angle.

From first timers to riding pros, we have a rafting rodeo experience suited for just about anyone on the river. Follow the lead of Glacier’s best guides as you paddle through rapids, take in the spectacular views and discover the wonders of the Flathead River on the adventure that’s right for you.

Discover more about our collection of possibilities to adventure with us today, so you can witness first hand the frontier fun with Glacier’s most well-known rafting company.
